Get your Slack tokens
Open Slack in your browser and extract two values from DevTools.
  1. Go to app.slack.com in Chrome/Edge and sign in
  2. Open DevTools: F12 or Cmd+Option+I
  3. Go to Application tab → Cookies → https://app.slack.com
  4. Find cookie named d — its value starts with xoxd-. Copy the full value.
  5. Go to the Console tab, type: document.cookie.match(/xoxc-[^;]+/)?.[0] and press Enter. Copy the result.
3

From Console — starts with xoxc-
